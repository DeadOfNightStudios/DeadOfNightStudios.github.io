Humans, a vastly diverse race, gave the realm the Arcane Sorceries and brought many of the advanced medicine and weapons along with them. Their race relies on intelligence and strength to build great empires and wage wars for land, glory, and wealth.

Dwarves are a small, stout, strong, and resourceful race that dwell deep beneath the ground building magnificent fortresses and crafting powerful items.

Elves are a tall, thin, agile, and spiritually inclined race that live within the great forests that cover the realm. It is because of the direct actions of the Elves that the trees, plants, and wildlife flourish and thrive. Many healing powers and spiritual enlightenment is spread from the Elves to the realm.

Drow, kin to the Elves, were forced underground long ago due to the results of warring kingdoms. Their white hair and dark skin easily distinguishes them from their Elven brothers and sisters. They rely on their agility and cunning to survive in the dark depths of the realm. Walking a different path than the Elves that walk the surface, the Drow still share their spiritual inclination. Just for a vastly different spiritual goal.

Orcs are a large, burly, brutish race that relies solely on their strength to solve their issues. Isolated to the Wildlands continent the Orcs live among nature pursuing conquest.

Little is known of the Spectres as they are a secretive and very powerful race. Most of the seen Spectres are rouge, separated, or enslaved. Their large lanky figures with their odd teal or purple skin makes them stand out in cities. Known for their Void Sorcery and Stealth skills the Spectres are feared across the land as elite hunters.
